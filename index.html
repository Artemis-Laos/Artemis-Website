They send it. It lands ticketed and clear where the food gets made. Your staff runs the room instead of running the order pad back and forth.

✦ THE ARTEMIS QR GUIDE

How Artemis QR works.

From the table to the kitchen to the bill — and how you run all of it yourself.

✦ FOR YOUR DINERS

From seat to served, in five taps.

Step 01

A code for every table

Each table gets its own QR code linked to your menu. A diner scans it and the menu opens on their phone instantly — no app, no sign-up.

Step 02

Order right at the table

They browse the menu, pick what they want, and tap Order — no waiting to catch a server's eye.

Step 03

Straight to the kitchen

The order lands in the kitchen the second it's placed. Your chef prepares it and the food is served — no handwritten tickets lost on the way.

Step 04

Pay when they're ready

When they're done, they tap to check the bill and pay at the counter — clear, itemised, no surprises.

Step 05

Done

That's the whole journey. Faster tables, fewer mistakes, and staff free to run the room instead of the order pad.

✦ FOR YOUR BUSINESS

You stay in full control.

Set up your menu

Add your dishes, photos, and prices once, and group them into categories. Your branded ordering page is ready to print as table codes.

Run it from your panel

Adjust prices, add or hide dishes, and watch every order arrive live — all from your own admin control panel. No need to call Artemis to change a thing.
